## The Honest Answer: It Depends (But Here Are Real Numbers)

Every business owner who's considered custom software has asked this question. And every agency gives the same frustrating answer: "it depends." That's true — but it's not helpful. So here are actual price ranges based on what we've built at iOLab Digital over the past 12 years.

## The Three Tiers of Custom App Development

### Tier 1: Focused Solution ($15,000 - $30,000)

This is a single-purpose tool that does one thing really well. Think of it as replacing one specific SaaS product.

**Examples:**
- Custom booking and scheduling system
- Client portal with project tracking
- Internal dashboard with custom reporting
- Branded loyalty or rewards app
- Automated email marketing system

**What you get:**
- 3-4 month build timeline
- Web application (responsive, works on all devices)
- Core features built to your specification
- Basic integrations (payment processing, email, etc.)
- 30-day post-launch support

![Custom App Development Pricing Tiers — Basic, Standard, and Enterprise Comparison](https://iolab.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/images/generated/blog/pricing-tier-breakdown.png)

### Tier 2: Integrated Platform ($30,000 - $60,000)

This replaces multiple SaaS tools with one unified platform. Most businesses that come to us land here.

**Examples:**
- [Custom CRM](/services/custom-crm) with pipeline management, email automation, and reporting
- Field service platform with scheduling, dispatch, invoicing, and customer portal
- E-commerce platform with inventory management and marketing automation

**What you get:**
- 4-6 month build timeline
- Web + mobile application
- Multiple integrated modules
- Third-party integrations (accounting, payment, communication)
- [AI automation](/services/ai-automation) features (chatbot, smart notifications, etc.)
- 60-day post-launch support

### Tier 3: Enterprise-Grade System ($60,000 - $100,000+)

This is a comprehensive platform that runs a significant part of your business operations.

**Examples:**
- Full business operating system (CRM + project management + billing + customer portal + AI)
- Multi-location management platform with role-based access
- Custom marketplace or multi-sided platform

**What you get:**
- 6-12 month build timeline
- Web + native mobile apps (iOS and Android)
- Complex business logic and workflows
- Advanced AI integration
- Real-time data processing
- Role-based access control
- Ongoing development partnership

## What Affects the Price

Not all apps in the same tier cost the same. Here's what moves the needle:

**Increases cost:**
- Native mobile apps (iOS + Android) vs. responsive web app
- Complex business logic (approval workflows, multi-step processes)
- Real-time features (live updates, notifications, chat)
- Extensive third-party integrations
- Compliance requirements (HIPAA, financial regulations)
- Large-scale data migration from existing systems

**Keeps cost lower:**
- Web-only (no native mobile app needed)
- Clear, well-defined requirements from the start
- Standard integrations (Stripe, Twilio, SendGrid)
- Phased delivery (launch core features first, add the rest later)

## The Comparison That Matters: Custom vs. 5 Years of SaaS

The sticker price of custom development feels high until you compare it to what you're already spending.

**Typical small business SaaS stack:**
- CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce): $150-$500/month
- Scheduling (Calendly/Acuity): $20-$50/month
- Email marketing (Mailchimp/Klaviyo): $50-$300/month
- Project management (Monday/Asana): $30-$100/month
- Communication (Twilio/phone system): $50-$200/month
- Other tools: $50-$200/month

**Monthly total: $350-$1,350/month**
**5-year total: $21,000-$81,000**

And at the end of those 5 years, you own nothing. The prices went up every year. Your data is locked in their systems. And you're still paying.
