## Why Email Marketing Costs Vary So Wildly for Hospitality

Ask three different vendors what email marketing costs for a restaurant or hotel, and you'll get three wildly different answers. That's because "email marketing" covers everything from a free Mailchimp account to a $5,000/month agency retainer.

Here's a clear breakdown so you know exactly what you're paying for — and what you should be paying.

## The Three Tiers of Restaurant & Hotel Email Marketing

### Tier 1: DIY with a SaaS Platform ($0 - $300/month)

This is the most common starting point. You pick a platform, build templates yourself, and manage your own campaigns.

**Typical costs:**
- Mailchimp: Free for up to 500 contacts, $13-$350/month after that
- Constant Contact: $12-$80/month
- Klaviyo: Free for up to 250 contacts, $20-$150/month
- Toast Marketing (restaurants): $75/month (bundled with POS)

**What you get:** Basic templates, simple automations (welcome email, birthday), and open/click tracking.

**The catch:** You're spending 5-10 hours/week managing campaigns, designing emails, writing copy, and analyzing results. For a busy restaurant or hotel, that time has a real cost.

### Tier 2: Agency-Managed ($1,000 - $5,000/month)

An agency handles strategy, design, copywriting, and campaign management. You approve, they execute.

**Typical costs:**
- Small agency: $1,000-$2,000/month
- Mid-size agency with hospitality focus: $2,000-$4,000/month
- Full-service digital marketing agency: $3,000-$5,000/month

**What you get:** Professional templates, strategic campaign planning, A/B testing, monthly reporting, and someone who knows hospitality marketing.

**The catch:** You're still paying for the SaaS platform on top of agency fees. You don't own the templates or automations — if you leave the agency, you often start from scratch.

### Tier 3: Custom-Built Email System ($15,000 - $25,000 one-time)

A custom [email marketing system](/services/email-marketing) built specifically for your restaurant or hotel, integrated with your POS, reservation system, and [CRM](/services/custom-crm).

**Typical costs:**
- One-time build: $15,000-$25,000
- Monthly hosting and support: $200-$500/month
- No per-subscriber fees

**What you get:** Everything in Tier 2 plus full automation, POS integration, guest profile-based targeting, and [AI-powered](/services/ai-automation) personalization — all branded to your property.

## The Hidden Costs Nobody Talks About

### Per-Subscriber Pricing Adds Up Fast

A [restaurant](/industries/restaurants) with 5,000 email subscribers on Mailchimp pays $75/month. Sounds reasonable. But that same restaurant doing email, SMS, and automation on Klaviyo is paying $200+/month. A hotel with 20,000 past guests? $350-$500/month just for the platform.

Over 5 years, that's $12,000 to $30,000 — and you own nothing.

### Your Data Is Locked In

Every campaign you've run, every template you've built, every automation you've set up — it all lives in someone else's platform. Switch tools and you're starting over. That's not a feature; it's a lock-in strategy.

### Template Limitations Hurt Your Brand

Hospitality is a visual industry. Your emails should look as good as your restaurant or hotel. But template builders limit you to their components, their fonts, and their layouts. The result? Your emails look like every other restaurant's emails.

## The Smart Approach for Hospitality

For a single-location restaurant doing basic newsletters, a $50-$100/month SaaS tool is fine. Don't overcomplicate it.

For multi-location restaurants, boutique hotels, and hospitality groups serious about guest engagement — a custom system pays for itself. You get POS-integrated campaigns (email about items they've actually ordered), reservation-triggered sequences (pre-arrival, post-stay), and genuine personalization that generic tools can't touch.
